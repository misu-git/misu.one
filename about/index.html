About me

Advocate for free markets

Economics and computers have always piqued my attention since I was a youngster.

When I initially learned about Bitcoin in 2016, I was mostly indifferent, concentrating on the currency rather than the technology.

However, when I returned it in early 2019, I became aware of new possibilities emerging within the areas of blockchain and ownership, as well as the inevitable societal transformation that would result.

That year, a buddy introduced me to Ethereum’s Solidity, and Smart Contracts have ruled my life ever since.

Prior to Smart Contracts, I studied applied sciences, then finance, banking, and stock markets.

I worked as an account manager for a brokerage business, where I learned about the technical aspects of trading.

By training customers, I was able to observe human nature at work in the stock market and the consequences of their actions.
