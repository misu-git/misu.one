The Proof of Burn (PoB) concept

Proof of burn is one of the various consensus mechanism methods used by a blockchain network to ensure that all participating nodes agree on the blockchain network’s real and valid state.

This algorithm is in place to prevent any cryptocurrency coin from being double-spent.

Proof of burn is based on the idea of miners “burning” the coins that provide them mining rights.
